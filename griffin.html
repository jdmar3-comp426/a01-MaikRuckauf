Learn about the Griffin.

The Griffin is a mythological creature with the body, tail, and back of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle. These creatures are often regarded as a symbol of power. It is featured in the coat of arms of many mdeieval kingdoms. Griffins are also believed to be protectors of treasures and in Greek and Roman texts, they were associated with gold deposits of Central Asia.
