Learn about the Cyclops.

The Cyclops is a mythological creature originating in Greek mythology and later Roman mythology. A Cyclops is a giant one-eyed creature. One of the most famouse stories about a Cyclops comes from Homers' Odyssey where they are a group of shepherds encountered by Odysseus. They are known for their giant size and massive strength and are often depicted as city-builders or blacksmiths.
