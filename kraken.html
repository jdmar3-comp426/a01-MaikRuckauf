Learn about the Kraken.

The Kraken is a legendary sea monster known for its gigantic size and octopus-like appearance. The legends of the Kraken may have stemmed from sightings of giant squids in our oceans. The Kraken has been the focus of sailers making their way across the North Atlantic and especially sailors from Nordic countries.
